Privacy Policy – LineLock

Effective date: December 2025

LineLock is designed to respect your privacy. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Information LineLock Uses

LineLock stores a small amount of local extension settings using Chrome’s built-in storage system:

  • Reading speed (words per minute)
  • Toolbar position
  • Onboarding and hint display state

This information is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted off your device.

Information LineLock Does Not Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Highlighted or read text
  • Personal identifiers
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Account or login information

Data Sharing

LineLock does not share data with third parties. There are no ads, analytics, trackers, or external services involved.

Network Activity

LineLock does not make any external network requests. All functionality runs locally within your browser.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
